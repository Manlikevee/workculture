About Work Culture
Helping businesses and individuals discover their excellence genes rocks our world.
Our Mission
To improve lives, influence excellence in the ethics and value system that define work both at organizational and individual levels by facilitating mind shifts and providing capacity growth interventions that ultimately enable business sustainability in organisations.
Our Vision
To be the foremost influence for the mindshift change that enables the adoption of sound Work Culture practice by Organisations and individuals in Nigeria and Sub Saharan Africa.


Building sustainable Businesses with lasting legacies
We are a Human Resource Advisory Company dedicated to the enrichment of sound Human Resource Practice in Organisations. We offer Advisory Services to Small & Medium sized Enterprises (SMEs) lending the expertise required to handle people matters with excellence.
Work Culture was incorporated in November 2021 under guiding principles which emphasize People, Values and the Impact a fusion of both can create for growth, not just for the Organisation but also for the individuals who work there.
Notably, the Culture of Work is fast evolving. While there are basic philosophical foundations that guide the practice of work, we acknowledge that changing times require some modification to attain or retain a distinct work place.
We believe that efficient execution of People and Values guided principle is the recipe for building sustainable Businesses with lasting legacies.

With an understanding of your business goals, we help your business identify opportunity areas that can be optimized using audits, strategy, people management, values, culture, capacity development and change management to achieve same.
If your business is struggling with employee retention, business structure, policies, procedures, capacity development of employees, organizational culture and employer brand issues, then we can help you.




We also believe that excellent organisations are because of excellent people.
At Work Culture, we are driven by the desire to see professional HR best practice as a way of life in organizational dynamics ultimately for short and long term benefits in productivity, resource efficiency, business space leadership, profitability, brand respect, corporate responsibility.
We also believe that excellent organisations are because of excellent people. Helping businesses and individuals discover their excellence genes rocks our world.
